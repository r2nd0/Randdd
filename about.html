About Us

We are a small group of students passionate about design, technology, and building useful things together. This site is a place to share who we are, what we work on, and how to reach us.

Our Mission

To learn by doing — turning simple ideas into real projects and improving our skills with every step. We focus on clean designs, readable code, and clear communication.

What We Do (Unordered List)

  • Design and build small web projects
  • Practice front-end development with HTML & CSS
  • Collaborate as a team and share what we learn

How We Work (Ordered List)

  1. Plan the idea and decide on the goal
  2. Sketch a simple design
  3. Write the HTML and CSS
  4. Test the pages in the browser
  5. Review the work as a team
